Controls

Place Troops

Your main goal is to command a growing army, defeat all enemy forces and keep your kingdom safe! Learning how to place your units well can make all the difference between victory and defeat:

Before each battle, you’ll see the layout of the enemy’s forces. Take a moment to plot your formation. Arrange your frontline fighters to absorb damage while ranged units stay protected in the rear.

Use different unit types to build a balanced army:

Swordsman: Great for quick attacks.

Axeman: Hits harder but slower.

Spearman: Good range for a melee unit.

Ranged Fighters

Archer: Basic ranged attacker.

Axegirl: Throws axes with strong impact.

Spearwoman: Long-range piercing attacks.

Cavalry

Mounted Axeman: Heavy damage on horseback.

Mounted Spearman: Charges from a distance.

Mages

Ladymage: Casts support and damage spells.

Heroes

Prince & Princess: Strong and inspiring leaders.

Paladin: Tough frontline hero.

Enchantress: Uses magical powers to turn the tide of battle.

Use Resources Wisely

Every time you win a battle, you’ll earn valuable silver that you can use to improve your troops. Upgrading your units boosts their health and attack power and sometimes unlocks new skills. Even a small upgrade can mean the difference between a narrow loss and a glorious victory. The smart move? Save up enough silver to power up your best fighters so they’re always ready for the toughest challenges ahead.

As you progress through quests, you’ll also receive chests full of awesome rewards like gold, silver, and precious gemstones. Keep in mind that some chests might take a little while to open—don’t worry, it’s worth the wait! You can open up to four chests at once, but if you need more room, you can watch a quick ad to unlock an extra slot. Each chest has a chance to drop rare items, including jade and sapphire, which are especially useful for forging stronger hero weapons. By stocking up on these rare materials, you’ll make your army more powerful and your heroes nearly unstoppable. Don’t miss out—open every chest you get, upgrade often, and watch your army grow stronger with every battle!

Game Modes

Remember, you can start in Campaign mode to unlock everything else. Once you’ve reached level 6, jump into Training to test new tactics, then head into Online PvP to challenge real opponents across the globe.

Kingdom Battle 3D has three exciting modes, each offering a unique way to master the game:

Campaign Mode: Start your journey here. Complete various levels to battle increasingly tough enemies, earn valuable rewards, and unlock new units. Campaign Mode helps you build the foundations for your army and strategy skills.

Training Mode (Unlocked after Campaign Level 6): Perfect to try out unit combinations and new strategies freely, without worrying about resources or losing rank.

Online PvP Mode (Unlocked after Campaign Level 6): Battle against players worldwide. Use smart tactics, upgrade your units, to earn special rewards and show your strategic talent
