Highway Racer Pro

By Unblocked Games

Picture a situation where you're commuting through a sea of cars in heavy traffic: every car is honking, and the red brake lights hurt your eyes. The heat radiating from the asphalt makes the situation even worse. You're desperately wondering if you'll arrive on time. We've all been there, haven't we? In such moments, have you ever fantasized about cutting through all the cars, performing crazy maneuvers, and even slightly bumping into the cars that brake all the time? Well, your dreams have come true! You can do all this and more in Highway Racer Pro, a terrific unblocked driving game with an insane amount of tuning options. If you're up for it, let's hit the road!

Controls

  • Gas: W or up arrow
  • Steer: AD or left and right arrow keys
  • S key or Spacebar: Brake
  • C: Change camera
  • Left Shift: Nitro
  • Esc: Pause
  • K: Honk
  • L: Toggle headlights

How to Play Highway Racer Pro

  • Choose your car in the garage, and tune it to your liking.
  • Pick a game mode and hit the road.
  • Drive as long as possible to earn coins.
  • Multiply your earnings with near misses, speeding over the limit, and going in the wrong direction. Everything a cop would stop you from doing in real life brings you points in this game! How cool is that?
  • You are allowed to bump into other cars lightly, and you'll re-spawn when it happens. Keep track of the red health bar at the bottom of your screen. The bigger the crash, the more it fills, bringing the end of the ride.

Game Modes

  • One Way: Drive on a one-way ride on the highway. Perfect to adjust to the gameplay and enjoy a less fussy ride.
  • Two-way: Driving in the wrong direction will bring you $$$. But, it's harder to keep the car in one piece in this mode compared to one-way.
  • Time Attack: You have a full 100 seconds to make crazy maneuvers.
  • Speed Bomb: If you drive too slow, the bomb in the car explodes after 10 seconds.
  • Multiplayer: Create a room to race with friends while cutting up other cars.

Garage and Tuning Options

There are 18 cars in the garage, and all are affordable, with the 100,000 coins given to you as you enter the game. We suggest you unlock the car you'd prefer before moving on to tuning. You can look at cars' torque, handling, brake, and speed levels in the garage before making any decision. Then, start your tuning journey.

  • Paint Your Car: Pick a color and shade from the color slider. For only 2500 coins, you can have any shade of any color.
  • Change Wheels: 17 different wheels are available. Each for 1000 coins.
  • Height and Camber Tuning: Set a combination of rear and front heightand cambers, spring force and damp levels to customize your vehicle's look. It's all free!
  • Upgrade Parts: Upgrade car parts such as piston, steering wheel, brakes, and turbocharger. You can also activate nitro from this menu. All parts can be upgraded to 5th level. And each upgrade is 1000 coins.
  • Add a Spoiler: This is great for making your car even fancier. 16 different spoilers that'll make your car look like a plane are available. Each spoiler cost 1000 coins.
  • Add Stickers: Buy stickers for the left and right doors of your car for a fully customized look.
  • Change the Color of Lights: Change the headlights and the color of the wheel smoke from this menu.
