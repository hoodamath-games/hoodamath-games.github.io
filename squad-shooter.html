Squad Shooter

By Unblocked Games

Ready to play a 3D shooter game without the frustration of complex controls and precise aiming? Look no further than Squad Shooter, an intuitive game designed for gamers of all skill levels. With laid-back controls, fun gameplay, and upgradable weapons and characters, you'll be hooked from the start.

One of the best things about Squad Shooter is its unblocked nature, making it accessible on restricted networks like schools and universities. Plus, the simple 3D visuals guarantee a smooth experience even on Chromebooks or with low-speed internet.

When you're looking to unwind and blow off some steam, Squad Shooter is the perfect choice. No need to worry about never-ending loading screens! Play Squad Shooter Unblocked now!

Controls

  • WASD or arrow keys to move and shoot
  • Mouse scroll wheel to zoom in/out

How to Play Squad Shooter

  • You'll take on the role of a solo shooter in a mining field overrun by outlaws.
  • Take out the enemy squad patrolling the area, and collect loot as you go.
  • One important feature to note is the circle around your character, which indicates your weapon's shooting range.
  • When an enemy enters this zone, your weapon will automatically aim and shoot for you.
  • So, all you need to worry about is keeping your health topped up by dodging bullets - let your weapon do the rest!
  • When you clear all the enemies, the exit gate will open either taking you to a new area in the level or to the end of the level depending on the difficulty.
  • There are more than 40 great levels including boss levels which you should try hard to complete.

Upgrade Characters

As you complete levels, you'll earn XP and characters. There are four characters to unlock. You can upgrade them up to the 5th level with your coins to improve their resilience to bullets. Characters names are as follows

  • Jim (Free)
  • Mike (unlocks at 3rd XP level)
  • Dustin (unlocks at 5th XP level)
