Papa's Burgeria

By Unblocked Games

Papa's Burgeria Unblocked is a cooking and time management game where you take charge of a bustling burger shop. Papa Louie is a busy guy whose fingers are in many culinary pies, or should I say, franchises. While juggling his restaurants, including Freezeria, Cheeseria and Cupcakeria, he doesn't have much time to onboard new employees. So grab your uniform, get behind the counter and train yourself to be a burger baron. You'll be taking orders, grilling patties and staking veggies to create the perfect burgers. Each customer has their own quirky preferences, and trust me, they're as picky as a food critic with a magnifying glass. So precision and speed are key to keeping them happy! With each satisfied customer, you'll earn tips and climb the ranks of the burger biz.

Controls

  • Use your mouse to interact with the grill, toppings, and orders.
  • Click and drag items to assemble the burgers as ordered.

How to Play Papa's Burgeria

  • Take customer orders at the front counter (order station) and check their preferences.
  • Head to the grill to cook patties. Watch the timer to avoid undercooking or burning them! You can see how the customer wants their burger patties.
  • Add the requested toppings and condiments in the correct order for each burger. Stack toppings with the precision of a brain surgeon is the hardest part. Don't let that cheese slide over the burger.
  • Deliver the burger and collect tips based on accuracy and speed.
  • As you progress through days in Burgeria, orders get more complex, customers become impatient, and the number of customers each day will increase. You'll need to juggle between orders and stations.

Upgrade the Burgeria

At the start of each day, you can visit the Upgrade Shop to spend the tips you've earned. Here's a sneak peek into what you can buy for your Burgeria.

  • Lobby Posters: Buy a few posters and hang them in the lobby. Not only do posters add a splash of style, but they also possess the mystical power to make customers chill longer before their patience starts to wane. It's like giving them a cozy waiting room experience.
  • Lobby Items: Apart from posters, there are devices (like arcade machines) that can make your customers wait with the patience of monks.
  • Doorbell: Install a doorbell, and voila! Now, every time someone new walks in, you'll get a little jingle. While grilling patties, you won't have to check if a customer comes in every two seconds. It's so practical.
  • Grill Timers: These aren't your average kitchen timers. They're your personal burger-flipping oracles, telling you precisely when to flip and when to whisk those patties off the grill to burger perfection.
  • Heat Lamp: Once patties are done, they go under this lamp, which isn't just any heat lamp. It's like a cozy blanket for your burgers, keeping them warm.
