Infiltrating The Airship

By Unblocked Games

Henry Stickmin just can’t catch a break. One moment, he’s stealing a giant diamond and the next, he’s caught by the government. But instead of rotting in prison (again), he’s given an offer that he cannot refuse. The mission is to take down the notorious Toppat Clan, a gang of sky-high criminals; then, he’ll walk free. Sounds doable? Well… considering this is Henry Stickmin, things are bound to go off the rails.

In Infiltrating the Airship, you’ll help Henry break into a flying fortress filled with high-tech security, questionable gadgets, and criminals who definitely don’t want him there. Will you sneak in like a master spy, go in guns blazing, or accidentally eject yourself into the sky like a human rocket? Every choice you make leads to a different (and usually ridiculous) outcome. This mission could make Henry a hero, but knowing him, there’s also a very good chance he’ll end up falling out of the airship with nothing but regret. And there's only one way to know. Play Henry Stickmin Infiltrating the Airship Unblocked now and see what happens.

Controls

Computers: Click to select your next move.

How to Play Henry Stickmin: Infiltrating the Airship

  • Choose how Henry infiltrates the airship: stealth, force, or complete nonsense are all valid strategies (kind of). If you choose headphones and mics, he'll have a hacker on the other side of the phone helping (or hampering) in his duty.
  • Left-click to select your moves and watch the infiltrating the airship duty unfold. Sometimes gadgets save the day, and sometimes they just backfire spectacularly. Encounter wild characters, unexpected betrayals, and the kind of cartoon physics that makes no sense.
  • Feel free to pick different paths to uncover multiple endings, go back to previous decisions and try to change things. Eventually, he'll succeed or fail in one of the unique endings. It's up to you!

Explore The Henry Stickmin Series

If you enjoy guiding Henry through ridiculous decisions, check out Stealing the Diamond, Fleeing the Complex, or any game where failure is just as fun as success! Will Henry finally prove himself as a master infiltrator, or will he become an exemplary tale of why you should always read the instruction manual on high-tech gadgets? There’s only one way to find out: get infiltrating!
