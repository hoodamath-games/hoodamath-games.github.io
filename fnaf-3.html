FNAF 3

By Unblocked Games

Five Nights at Freddy's 3 (FNAF 3) is an unblocked survival horror game that puts you in a dangerous situation.

30 years after the unfortunate events in the first two games, a horror attraction now opens in an amusement park. Fazbear's Fright: The Horror Attraction is a replica place of the old Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. You'll see dolls, animatronics, and pictures of them everywhere. You say to yourself "Gone are the days when being a night security guard was dangerous around animatronics". And then you apply for this job and actually get accepted! Great news! Isn't it?

So, the mission is straightforward: spend 5 consecutive nights in Fazbear's Fright from 12 AM to 6 AM. Your job is simple in theory: keep an eye on the security cameras and monitor the building. But things start to go wrong when you realize that something sinister has been brought back. The haunted atmosphere and dark surroundings create a tense environment.

Springtrap, an animatronic from the past, lurks in the shadows, waiting for the right moment to strike.

You'll need to rely on your camera system, audio devices, and ventilation to survive. But the systems can fail, and when they do, you must reboot them quickly.

FNAF 3 Unblocked is a test of patience and strategy. Every move you make matters, and every mistake could be your last. The horror builds as the nights get harder, with hallucinations, broken systems, and the constant threat of Springtrap.

Controls

  • Mouse: To Look Around
  • Left-Click: Use Camera/Maintenance Panels
  • Spacebar: To Seal Vents

How to Play FNAF 3

  • In Five Nights at Freddy's 3, you are trapped in a haunted attraction where your only defense is a camera system, audio devices, and ventilation.
  • The main goal is to survive the night by managing these systems and avoiding jumpscare moments by Springtrap.
  • Use the cameras to keep track of Springtrap's movement through the building.
  • You will need to watch closely because Springtrap can move quickly, and missing his position could lead to your demise.
  • You can toggle between camera modes in this game, giving you more places to control. The good thing is you don't need a flashlight in this third game of FNAF.
  • You can lure Springtrap away by turning on the audio system. Play sounds in specific rooms to distract him.
  • The ventilation system is crucial to prevent hallucinations.
  • If it fails, you will start seeing phantom animatronics that aren't real but can still scare you and cause you to lose control.
  • Throughout the game, systems will fail—cameras, audio, and ventilation. You need to quickly reboot them from the maintenance panel, but each reboot takes time. In the meantime, you'll be vulnerable to Springtrap.
  • So, stay sharp and react quickly to survive 5 nights in this bloody place.

Developer

Five Nights at Freddy's 3 was developed by Scott Cawthon, the creator of the entire FNAF series. Cawthon has designed the game with a focus on tension and suspense.
