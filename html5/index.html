Car Football

Car Football is a fun twist on football – you’re playing with your card in a 2D landscape, trying to bounce the ball into the opponent’s net. You can play against another player in local co-op, or you can attempt to beat all 30 levels that will take you through a world tournament.

The opponents will be getting harder and harder with each level, so don’t think you’re through just because you beat the first few levels.

You can also unlock new cars to play with using coins, which you can earn by playing the campaign. Do you have what it takes to beat all the opponents?

Fire and Water

If you have a friend to play with, Fire and Water is the perfect test of cooperation and coordination. The two of you will have to navigate puzzles to escape each level – but each of you starts in a different spot and will have to take a different route to the exit. Along the way, you’ll have to help each other by unlocking pathways and disabling threats, as the game is all about team play.

Use WASD and the arrow keys to control both characters. There are 30 levels waiting for you, with increasingly complex puzzles to solve. Remember – the fireball cannot touch water, while the waterball cannot touch fire.

Crazy Stickman Physics

Play around with a ragdoll stickman in a physics-powered environment of Crazy Stickman Physics, throwing him down obstacle courses to deal as much damage as possible. With each attempt, you’ll be rewarded with coins and experience points, which you can use to unlock and purchase new vehicles, levels, and cosmetic items for your stickman. Use traps, guns, explosives, and other hazards to increase the damage – gaining you even more coins as a result!

Snake Football

You’ve played football, and you’ve played snake – but have you ever played Snake Football?

Snake Football is a game for two players (also playable against AI), where you try to score goals against your opponent – the twist being that you’re both playing as slithering snakes.

Use the mouse (or your finger if you’re playing on mobile), WASD, or the arrow keys to move your snake around and try to outplay your opponent. You can unlock a range of different skins for your snake using the money you earn for playing the game.

In the campaign mode, you’ll have to progress through 4 different football fields, with each one featuring 5 levels with increasing difficulty. Each match lasts 2 minutes, so make sure you score as many goals as possible in that time.

Run 3 Space

Test your agility in Run 3 Space – a game where quick reflexes are crucial. In this game, your mission is to run through a space tunnel while avoiding the gaps. Don’t worry about gravity – everything is a surface you can run on in this game, including the walls and the ceiling.

Collect diamonds on your way to the portal, which you can later use to unlock new playable characters. The game features 30 different levels, with many obstacles hiding on your way to victory.

To play, use the arrow keys to move left or right as your character automatically runs forward, and press the Space key to jump. You can hold the Space key to jump higher.

Police Chase Drifter

Can you escape the police with all the money in Police Chase Drifter? In this top-down drifting game, you’ll be running away from police cars trying to push you off the road while also attempting to collect all the money bags scattered around the city and make your escape. Each new level will place new, harder obstacles on your way, and you’ll have to adapt your playstyle if you want to win them all.

You can use the money you collect to purchase new vehicles – there are 22 different cars for you to choose from, including classic muscle cars and modern supercars. The controls are simple – all you have to do is use the WASD or the arrow keys to control your vehicle around.

Love Tester

The last one is a little different – Love Tester is a fun little game where you can enter the names of two people to see how compatible they are based only on the names. There is nothing complex about this game; there are no levels to beat or new characters to unlock. Simply type in whatever two names you wish, click Test, and see how compatible the two people are with each other!
