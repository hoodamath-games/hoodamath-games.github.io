Supermarket Simulator

By Unblocked Games

Supermarket Simulator Unblocked is a brand-new 3D game that puts you in charge of your very own store. Picture a small town filled with charming streets, friendly neighbors, and endless possibilities (also for profits). In the heart of this town, you get the chance to build and manage a supermarket from the ground up! Whether you’re cleaning windows, hauling boxes of fresh cupcakes and donuts, or chatting with customers, you’ll quickly discover that every day brings a new challenge.

We built the Supermarket Simulator so you can feel what it’s like to run a real store without leaving your computer. You’ll start by choosing an empty shop in need of a little love. The floors might be dusty and old papers might cover the windows. But don’t worry. Once you pull out your trusty brush and start cleaning, the space will soon sparkle. After that, you’ll decide on a name and tagline that shows off your style. Do you want to run a cozy bakery that focuses on sweet treats? Or maybe you dream of a large grocery with rows of fruits, vegetables, and drinks? The choice is yours to make!

Next, you’ll hunt for the best supplies, like cash registers, shelving units, and the perfect products to draw curious customers inside. You’ll also get to meet the local seller in town, who is always happy to trade goods and help your store become the top spot for snacks and essentials. It won’t take long before people start flocking to your supermarket, and soon you’ll hear the delightful chime of the door opening as customers step inside.

What sets Supermarket Simulator apart is how it lets you control every little detail. You can move items, adjust prices, and even strike up conversations with customers. If juggling all these tasks seems overwhelming, don’t panic! You can hire helping hands like a cashier to handle sales and a cleaner to keep everything tidy. Then, you can even open a second branch in the neighborhood. This free unblocked game offers a perfect blend of freedom, creativity, and responsibility of running a store, so you’re always finding something new to do. Plus, with colorful 3D visuals and a lively town setting, your store feels like a real place in a welcoming neighborhood.

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys: Move around
  • Shift: Sprint
  • Mouse: Look around
  • E: Interact with objects and people
  • Left-click: Stock your shelves
  • Tab: Open the main menu
  • Esc: Access settings
  • M: Open the map

How to Play Supermarket Simulator

Follow these simple steps to get started:

Pick Your Store

Walk on the town map. Select an available store location. Rent it and give your store a cool name and a catchy tagline. You can the sign color as well.

Clean the Store

After entering, Press Tab to open the main menu. Select Clean to grab your brush. Remove old papers (by pressing E while hovering over them) and sweep dust inside the store. Make sure every corner looks tidy and clean.

Buy Supplies

Go outside and find the local seller in front of your store. Interact with them using E. Purchase important items like cash registers, shelves, and goods.

Build and Arrange

Return to your store. Press Tab and choose Build. Place your cash register and shelves wherever you like. You can rotate them and move around the store as well. Set up a nice layout so customers can move around easily.

Call Your Shipment

Open the menu again. Select Call Shipment to choose which goods you want to be delivered. Your boxes will appear outside the store. Carry them inside and stock your shelves with items like cupcakes, fish, drinks, and more. To stock your shelves, carry the boxes inside and left-click on the shelves to fill them with goods.

Open for Business

When you make everything ready, find the hanging sign on your door. Press E to interact with it and switch from Closed to Open. Shoppers will start coming in right away.

Manage and Interact

Run the cash register yourself or hire a cashier on the menu. Adjust prices by interacting with price tags. You'll see the cost and price on this menu. Keep the store clean by hiring a cleaner or doing it yourself. And don't forget to greet customers. If the business performs well, you can open a second supermarket by renting out another shop in town.

Game Features

  • Detailed Store Customization: Choose your shop’s name and style. Place registers, build shelving, and arrange goods for the perfect layout.
  • Lots of Goods: Stock anything from tasty cupcakes and donuts to fresh fruits, veggies, and savory meats.
  • Hiring System: Need to focus on business strategy? Hire employees to do the daily tasks like cleaning and operating the register.
  • Interactive Gameplay: Talk with customers, tweak prices, and offer change at the register. You decide how hands-on you want to be.
  • Open-World Town: Walk around, buy new things, meet people, and come back to make your store better every day.

Tips & Tricks

  • Manage Your Time: Cleaning up early makes your store look more inviting when it’s time to open.
  • Shop Wisely: Keep an eye on which goods sell best so you don’t overspend on unwanted products taking up space on shelves.
  • Adjust Price: If your customers look frustrated, try lowering prices. But if lines are always full, experiment with small price bumps.
  • Sprint Outside: Use Shift to speed up whenever you’re moving boxes or heading to the seller. This helps save a lot of time.
  • Use the Menu: The Tab key is your best friend! Use it often to clean, build, or call in fresh shipments.
