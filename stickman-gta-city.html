Stickman GTA City

By Unblocked Games

Stickman GTA City is an unblocked 3D action game available to play directly on your web browser at Unblocked Games. The game offers an open-world experience where you take on the role of a member of the Dixie Mafia, completing missions, taking on challenges, and navigating a chaotic cityscape filled with opportunities for action and adventure. Whether it's stealing and driving fast cars, outsmarting the police, messing up with random pedestrians, or piloting a helicopter, Stickman GTA City is the unblocked game you need. As you play further, you might notice that Stickman GTA City draws inspiration from iconic games like GTA Vice City, offering similar mechanics with its unique twist. You play as a stickman entrenched in the world of organized crime, taking orders from the boss of the Dixie Mafia and carrying out missions ranging from vehicle challenges to intense combat. The game's expansive map and diverse activities will provide you with endless fun.

Controls

  • W, A, S, D: Move your character.
  • Spacebar: Jump.
  • LMB: Look Around
  • Z: Aim
  • RMB or Ctrl: Attack
  • Shift: Sprint.
  • C: Crouch
  • F: Enter or exit vehicles.
  • 1-9 or Q key: Switch Weapons
  • R: Reload your weapon.

Follow Mafia Missions

As a member of the Dixie Mafia, you'll follow orders from your boss to complete a variety of missions. These include:

  • Taking on rival groups to secure key locations like farms, cargo shipments or escape houses
  • Stealing and delivering vehicles to specific garages.
  • Escaping from the police during high-stakes chases

And these missions are only a small part of what you can experience in Stickman GTA City. As you progress, you'll reveal the deep story behind Dixie Mafia and grab the cash rewards.

Explore the GTA City

Looking for extra challenges outside of missions? The game offers plenty of side activities that you can explore. One of the highlights of Stickman GTA City is the freedom to explore the city and use a variety of vehicles. You can:

  • Drive cars, motorbikes, ATVs and more to navigate through busy streets. You can complete daring stunts and drive through speed cameras for bonus cash. Explore the city to discover hidden rewards and surprises.
  • You can find helicopters and tanks on the map or create one using a cheat on Stickman's phone. You get to pilot helicopters and get a bird's-eye view of the action to explore the city all over. You can also control tanks to dominate any challenging situation, such as missions or escapes.

Navigate on Minimap

Located at the top-left corner of the screen, the minimap is your ultimate tool for finding key locations. Use it to:

  • Navigate to mission start points.
  • Locate weapon pick-up areas and helicopters.
  • Plan your escape routes during high-pressure situations.
  • The minimap ensures you always know where to go, keeping the game smooth and intuitive.

Customize Stickman

Stickman GTA City offers options to choose from different characters such as Hunter, Feist, Reaper, Ninja, Shooter, Sinister and others. Each character has unique stats. Head to the main menu to unlock them. Pick a character with higher stamina and power for extended action sequences. Or perhaps you can select a character with increased health levels to withstand tougher challenges.

Health and Medkits

Managing your health is crucial in Stickman GTA City. If your health level hits zero, you have to start over that mission. So when it runs low, use a medkit to restore it. These medkits are essential for surviving intense encounters and completing challenging missions. Keep an eye on your health bar and be ready to heal when the situation demands. When you're out of medkits, watch a short ad to get 3 medkits.

Earn and Spend Cash

Money plays a central role in Stickman GTA City. Earn cash by:

  • Completing missions assigned by your Mafia boss.
  • Participating in hidden activities and challenges throughout the city.

Once you've built up your fortune, spend your earnings on:

  • Unlocking new characters with enhanced abilities.
  • Stocking up on weapons and resources.

The more cash you have, the more options you unlock for dominating the city.

Weapons and Combat
