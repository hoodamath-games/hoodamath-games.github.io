FPS Strike

By Unblocked Games

FPS Strike Unblocked is an online multiplayer shooter game with TD and FFA game modes, 4 great maps, 18+ weapons and realistic shooting mechanics. Grab your Chromebooks, create a room and enjoy the game on your browser.

Controls

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Fire: Left Mouse Click
  • Aim: Right Mouse Click
  • Jump: Space
  • Change Weapon: 1, 2, 3, 4 or Mouse scroll
  • Reload: R
  • Crouch: C
  • Show Scoreboard: Hold Tab
  • Weapon Shop: B
  • Pause: Escape

Maps

  • Poolday: Guess what? Yes, the map is set on a pool area. Poolday map offers a mix of wide-open spaces and tight corridors. Good for both intense firefights and long range shooting.
  • Dust: A desert town map with narrow alleys and marketplaces. Offers long sightlines for sniping.
  • Aztec: This map is set in a museum filled with artifacts and ancient architecture. Explore long hallways and lots of stairs and admire artifacts while shooting. Keep the museum safe by kicking out the invaders.
  • Italy: Probably too charming for a village map of an FPS game. But you should definitely give it a try. We believe you'll enjoy the battle thrill blending with the visual feast formed by marketplaces and wine cellars of Italy map.
  • Ice World: Simplest map of the game regarding details but has lots of rooftops for sniping and open arena battles. Perfect for the players who focus on the battle rather than the surroundings.

Weapons

FPS Strike features a grand weapon shop with various guns. If you're new to the FPS games, here's the ultimate guide to help you choose the right weapon for the right situation. We'll start with pistols, move on to rifles, SMGs, and shotguns, and finish with some other powerful options.

Pistols

  • USP-S: Silencer for stealth and high accuracy, but lower damage and smaller magazine size. (Free)
  • Elite: Dual-wielding with high rate of fire and magazine capacity, but lower accuracy and high recoil. (600 coins)
  • P228: Good damage and accuracy, moderate rate of fire, but smaller magazine size and moderate recoil.(600 coins)
  • Deagle: High damage and long-range accuracy, but low rate of fire, high recoil, and small magazine size. (650 coins)
  • Five Seven: High magazine capacity and low recoil, but lower damage and less effective at long range. (750 coins)

Rifles

  • M4A1: High accuracy, moderate damage and rate of fire, but lower damage compared to some rifles and moderate recoil. (3100 coins)
  • AUG: Built-in scope for precision, high accuracy, but higher cost and slower movement speed when aiming. (3500 coins)
  • SG550: High accuracy and damage with a built-in scope, but lower rate of fire, high recoil, and expensive. (4200 coins)
  • SG552: High rate of fire, good damage and accuracy, but higher recoil and expensive. (3500 coins)
  • Scout: High mobility, decent damage and accuracy, but lower damage compared to AWP and requires precise aim. Very cost-effective for skilled players. (2750 coins)
  • AWP: One-shot kill potential, extremely high accuracy and damage, but very slow rate of fire, high recoil, and expensive. Good for attacking but not for defense in FFA mode. (4750 coins)

SMGs

  • Mac-10: High rate of fire and mobility, effective at close range, but low damage, high recoil, and ineffective at long range. (1400 coins)
  • MP5: Good rate of fire, accuracy, and moderate damage, but lower damage compared to rifles and moderate recoil. (Free)
  • P90: Very high rate of fire, large magazine capacity, and good mobility, but lower damage, high recoil, and less effective at long range. (2350 coins)

Shotguns

  • M3: High damage at close range, controlled firing, but very slow rate of fire, ineffective at long range, and low magazine capacity. (1700 coins)
  • XM1014: Semi-automatic with faster firing rate and high damage at close range, but lower accuracy, ineffective at long range, and expensive. (3000 coins)

Other Weapons

  • M249: Very high magazine capacity and rate of fire, effective for suppressive fire, but high recoil, slow movement speed, and the most expensive gun in the FPS Strike. (5750 coins)
  • Grenade: Deals area damage, effective for clearing multiple enemies, but limited carry capacity and requires precise timing and aim. (Free)
  • Melee: Great for close combats and when you're out of ammo. Should be used as a last resort. (Free)
