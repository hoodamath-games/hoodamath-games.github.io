  • Left Click: Interact with doors, lights, and camera menus.

How to Play Five Nights At Poppy

Your shift begins quietly enough in a claustrophobic security room. A handful of monitors keep watch on the hospital's corridors, patient rooms, storage closets, and even a morgue room. In theory, your job is just to keep an eye out for intruders. But the real threat is Poppy. She roams the halls, ready to jump at any sign of weakness, like a camera offline or a door left unguarded.

When a camera feed becomes static or flickers wildly, hold down your click to repair it. If it stays broken for a long time, you'll lose power throughout the building. That means no lights and no doors, which makes for a very, very scary and vulnerable situation. Glance at two corridors on the left and right sides as well. If you see movement or hear a suspicious noise or giant white eyes watching you, shut the door before you get a face-to-face encounter with that eerie grin. Each passing night, Poppy grows bolder and trickier, so you'll need to sharpen your reflexes. Stay focused if you want to last until morning.

In short, you'll be juggling multiple tasks at once: keeping an eye on cameras, fixing them when they spark, and managing limited power so you don't run out of electricity just when Poppy decides to pay you a visit.
