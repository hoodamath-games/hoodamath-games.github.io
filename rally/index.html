Rally Champion

An interesting aspect of this car rally racing game is that you can only control your vehicle with two keys - left and right arrows. So, you have to work on the best strategy for maneuvering your car to surpass your competitors.

You will be able to drive through as many as 50 different maps designed in various settings. However, you will have to gradually unlock them by finishing a race as at least a third participant.

In order to achieve this, you will have to pass other cars on the road without hitting them and without landing in the green area, as in this way, your vehicle will slow down. 

Interestingly, you can collect additional coins to buy new cars in this rally racer game by playing simple minigames, like guessing the number, matching the cards, or beating a maze challenge.

Offroad Rally

If you’re into more muddy rally car race games unblocked, this one should be right up your alley! Here, you will be able to drive on off-road racetracks with lots of bumps, rocks, wood pieces, and more.

In order to beat other contestants, you should maneuver against all the obstacles carefully and not let them slow you down. What’s more, you can get a temporary speed boost when your nitro is fully charged.

As you play this rally car race game, you will see lots of blue coins with an F written on them. By collecting them and winning races, you can get enough money to upgrade your existing truck or purchase a new one.

Monster Truck Dirt Rally

This is a perfect suggestion for players looking for free rally games with monster trucks! You will have the opportunity to participate in a race of giant monsters regardless of rain or shine.

There are three game modes to select from: Career, Challenge, and Offroad. In the first one, you will be simply racing with other contestants and trying to win - if you don’t arrive as the first participant, you lose!

In the Challenge mode, you will have an endurance bar and if it drops down to zero, you will be arrested by the police.

The last mode allows you to drive your monster truck in a freestyle manner on offroad tracks. You will just have to pass through ramps or other obstacles to mark all the checkpoints and proceed to the next level without any other participants in sight.
