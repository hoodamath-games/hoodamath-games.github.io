Kingdom Rush Frontiers

By Unblocked Games

Before the Game of Thrones craze, an epic comic book full of soldiers, towers, barracks, heroes, and dragons captured millions of hearts. So much so that a flash game was born—Kingdom Rush: Frontiers. You'll step into the shoes of the General in the King's forces and unfold the Kingdom Rush story once again. So, lead your troops through deserts and jungles to confront Lord Malagar (the boss enemy). You must defend your Kingdom before the blood meets the sand.

Kingdom Rush: Frontiers is an unblocked tower defense game with a great narrative and detailed gameplay. As you conquer new lands, you must protect your towns from waves of sieges. Strategically build defensive towers along the road to stop enemy attacks. Your people needs you! Start playing now!

Controls

Use the left mouse button to interact and navigate.

How to Play Kingdom Rush Frontiers Unblocked

  • Start by choosing a level of difficulty based on your experience in strategy games: casual, normal or veteran.
  • Nomadic desert tribes attack your precious land in ancient Hammerhold. Build defense towers by clicking on the suitable places. Choose from 4 basic tower types, which are
    • Archer Tower: Good Rate of Fire
    • Barracks: Soldiers block enemies
    • Mages Guild: High Damage armor piercing
    • Artillery: Deals area damage
  • The goal is to not to let any of the enemies enter your castle. So strategically position your towers, utilize your hero and overcome waves of attacks.
  • When your units start getting defeated, you can call reinforcements to block and fight enemies.
  • You can also use rain or fire from the skies. But use it as the last resort because the smoke and heat will not disperse away quickly after the rain of fire.

Train Your Hero

Your hero, Alric - the General of the Kings Forces- has extensive combat skills compared to other soldiers in command. You can position him where he is needed in the battlefield. After winning a few battles and gain experience, go to the hero room and train him to improve his skills.

There are 5 skills that can be improved up to the third level: swordsmanship, armor, toughness, flurry and sand warriors.

As you progress, you can unlock a range of unique heroes, each with their own special abilities:

  • Alric: the default hero, a champion and a swordsman
  • Mirage: An assassin with precision and speed
  • Cronan: A beastmaster who can rule animals and beasts
  • Captain BlachThorne: A pirate with extensive looting skills
  • Nivus: The grand wizard with magic missiles
  • Dierdre: A priestess with holy light to heal your units
  • Grawl: A giant Stone that throws huge boulders
  • Sha'tra: A sky hunter who throws glaives
  • Ashbite: Dragon with a blazing Breath
