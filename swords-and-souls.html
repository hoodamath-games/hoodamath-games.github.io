Swords and Souls

By Unblocked Games

Meet your new trainer, Sir Scarow! You'll have the best time together in your journey to becoming the best fighter in SoulTown! Swords and Souls Unblocked is a fun RPG game where you train your fighter to show your skills in SoulTown's Arena.

If you like the charming art style and gameplay of Rogue Soul, you'll definitely enjoy this flash game that takes place in the same universe. When it comes to training newbies, We may not be as talented as Sir Scarow. But we'll do our best to teach you how to master Swords and Souls on this page. Let's begin with controls.

Controls

  • Mouse: To interact and navigate menus
  • Arrow keys or WASD: To train and dodge in mini-games

How to Play Swords and Souls

  • In Swords and Souls, you start by creating a hero. Choose your name and customize the appearance first.
  • Then, either follow the tutorial by Sir Scarow (your trainer) or skip it.
  • Your main goal is to train your hero and level up to increase your stats.
  • Once your character is strong enough, you can fight in the Arena against waves of enemies.
  • As you win battles, you earn gold, XP and clovers.
  • These will allow tou to buy better equipment and skills.
  • You can also build a house, learn new abilities, and unlock special achievements along the way.

Navigate in SoulTown

There are 6 places in Soul Town that you'll juggle between them all the time: Arena, Training Room, Shop, Hero Stats, Home and a Museum.

Training Room

This is a place where you play mini training games with Sir Scarow. You can play 5 types of games here to improve your Strength, Defense, Accuracy, Dodge and Critical. Each one is essential for progressing through tougher enemies. Upgrading the training room will allow you to train your hero faster.

Shop

You can buy and upgrade new melees, armors, boosters and potions here. It's good to visit when you have enough money after fights.

Hero

Here, you will improve your stats, talents, and skills with your XP points. Training is not enough on its own. You need to turn your XP points into special types of attack that will let you gain advantage over enemies in the Arena.

Arena

When you're strong enough, visit Arena to engage in the first fight of yours. There are 30 fights that you need to clear all the enemies in it. There is also a survival fight and a final battle at the end. Winning a battle will bring you a great amount of coins and clovers.

Museum

You can view your stats trophies and get your interest from the bank in the museum building. Also, a mushroom waits for you to turn your clovers into boosters.

Home

A lovely princess awaits you at home. When you come back from fights, use your souleons to build or renovate the home. That will make your girl happy!

Developer

Swords and Souls was developed by Soul Game Studio.
