What is 2 Player Games?

2-player games are a type of video game designed for two players to play at the same time. Some board games, card games, or online multiplayer games can be given as examples. These games can be played on the same device (computer, tablet or phone). Both players share the same screen (or split screen) and keyboard.

Two player games can be competitive, where they fight or try to outsmart each other, such as the classic game Tic Tac Toe. Or they can be cooperative, where they work together to reach common goals, like in Fire and Water.
