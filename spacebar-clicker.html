Spacebar Clicker

By Unblocked Games

How fast can you press your spacebar? Spacebar Clicker is an unblocked clicker game where you will often use the most loved key button. Your job is to earn the highest scores by pressing the spacebar or using the button on the screen. Hit it as much as possible and challenge your speed in this idle game!

How to Play Spacebar Clicker

You will see a wide spacebar key button on the screen. Click on this button and start to earn points. In the beginning, each click brings you +1 point. As you get boosters, this figure will increase. If you don’t like constantly clicking on the button or are tired of using it, you can use your keyboard’s spacebar. Or, if you want to double the score you earn, use both of them at the same time. The choice is yours! You can even try to increase your pressing speed by trying different combinations with different fingers.

Monkey, Boomer Mom and Many More

  • The more you click, the more you earn scores. And these scores will enable you to get boosters.
  • They will help you get more scores in a second or per click. For example, getting the Monkey booster will earn you one score every 5 seconds. As it doesn’t know the space key, it will press all the keyboards randomly. When it hits the spacebar, it will get you points.
  • On the other hand, there is a Boomer Mom. She doesn’t know how to open a Word document, but she can earn you 3 points in a second.
  • Gen Z Kid, Keyboard Upgrade, Angry Influencer and many more are waiting for you to unlock them. We leave the discovery of others to you!

Try Different Combinations with Your Fingers

To press the key quickly, you can create it in your way. The method we use most is to use the button with our index finger while simultaneously pressing the button on the screen with the mousepad. You are free to generate your combinations considering our sample. In time, you will get faster! Additionally, the idle points you get with your boosters will also take you very far.
