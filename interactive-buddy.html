Interactive Buddy

By Unblocked Games

Click on the gray Buddy and generate money! Interactive Buddy is an unblocked arcade game where you punch, poke and slam a gray ragdoll. There is a character called "Buddy". Each time you interact with this guy, you will earn money.

Use this money to unlock new items, game modes and skins for ultimate joy. Thus, you can determine your own gaming experience in this clicker game. Try new kinds of interactions with the various options at your disposal!

Controls

  • Mouse: Interact with the Buddy

Basics of Interactive Buddy

  • When you enter the game, you will see a little guy on the screen. This gray ragdoll's name is "Buddy."
  • We can almost hear you asking, "What will I do with this character?" Your main objective in this clicker game is to engage with this Buddy by clicking on him.
  • Throw grenades at him or beat him up using your bare hands. With each interaction, you will get money!
  • You can view how much money you have from the dollar icon at the bottom left of the screen.
  • You are free to purchase unique weapons, unlock new game modes and get new skins from the bar at the top of the screen.

Item Store

There are lots of explosives, godpower, hand and object items in the Item Store. From here, you can choose the item you will attack with the ragdoll. There are grenades, Molotov cocktails, bowling balls, missiles and many more weapons and objects.

You can also pick a hand attack style such as fist or tickle. You can hose him down. Additionally, you can adjust the gravity vortex using godpowers. The choice is all yours in this arcade game!

Skin Store

Don't worry, and you don't have to interact with this gray Buddy all the time. You can also unlock new characters from the "Skin Store". You can find famous figures here. We will leave you the names of the skins below for you! Our favorite character is definitely Teletubby.
