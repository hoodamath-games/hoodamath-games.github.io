Nova Craft

By Unblocked Games

Nova Craft Unblocked is all about creativity, discovery and alchemy. You start with just four basic elements— water, fire, wind and earth. By combining them, you discover new things from plants, animals, minerals, concepts and even the universe itself.

It's a simple concept once used in games like Little Alchemy and Infinite Craft, but this one comes with even more objects to discover and faster progression. Once you start exploring, it's hard to stop. With hundreds of thousands of possible combinations and over 3000 objects, Nova Craft keeps your curiosity alive all the time. So, piece together the world in your own way now!

Controls:

  • Click and drag to move items around.
  • Combine items by dropping them on top of each other.
  • Right-click on items to clear them from the board.

How to Play Nova Craft

  • Start with the four basics: wind, water, fire, and earth.
  • Mix two elements to create something new.
  • For example, if you combine fire with water, you can unlock items like steam, opal, lava lamp and others.
  • There may be multiple products of two elements. Combine the same elements a few times to explore all the products of them.
  • Each new discovery is saved in your library for future use. You can keep track of them at the right side of your screen.
  • You can sort your library by name, time or emoji. You can also search for an item by typing.

Creativity Box For Faster Progress

The creativity box in Nova Craft is a game-changer for faster and smoother crafting.

Instead of manually dragging and dropping every single item in your library, you can use the creativity box to combine them faster.

To use it, place one item in the creativity box and then click on other discovered items on the left side of the screen. This allows you to experiment with multiple combinations quickly without the hassle of constantly dragging elements. It's perfect for when you're deep into the game and want to explore combinations faster. The creativity box helps streamline the process and keep your momentum going. If you set your mind on unlocking all 3,000+ items in the game, you need to use the box and leave no combinations unmade.

Save, Reset or Clear Discoveries

  • Save your game: Your progress in Nova Craft will be saved in the browser cache. However, if you'd like to download a save code, go to settings on the bottom left and click on "save game state". This will download a file that you upload next time and continue where you left off in any browser or computer.
  • Reset your progress: If you'd like to start over the game, click on the reset button on the left bottom of your screen. This will clear your library, and you'll be left with the 4 initial elements.
  • Clear Discoveries: If you don't want to reset the whole game but clear a few discoveries, click on the trash bin icon on the right bottom and choose the items in your library that you'd like to delete.
