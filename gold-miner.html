Gold Miner

By Unblocked Games

Play as a miner and dig the valuable gold under the ground! Gold Miner is an unblocked arcade game where you mine gold, diamonds and mystery bags using a claw. The claw will automatically swing back and forth.

You need to lower it and catch the valuable items from the earth. When you successfully reel the claw, all the spoils are yours in this skill game. Keep in mind that you have a limited time!

Controls

  • Down Arrow Key: Drop the Claw
  • Up Arrow Key: Toss Dynamite

How to Play Gold Miner

  • Your main objective in this arcade game is to dig the earth and get as many gold pieces as you can before the time is up.
  • To pass a level, you must reach a certain amount of money. Otherwise, you will need to restart from level 1.
  • The claw moves automatically. You must send it down when it points to exactly one gold piece.
  • All you need to do is lower the claw by properly targeting the gold pieces or other valuable items.
  • When the claw catches a piece successfully, it will automatically go up. However, reeling up will take time, too!

Avoid Catching the Rocks

There are rocks under the ground besides the gold, diamonds and mystery bags. Catching them will not earn you so much money. On the contrary, it will make you waste your time.

Our mascot Our Platform recommends you to avoid catching the rocks. Of course, this will not be that easy because they generally take up a large space under the earth. Try your best not to haul them up!

Get a Dynamite

You can visit the shop between levels. There, you can purchase dynamites, strength drinks, lucky clovers, rock collectors books, and diamond polishes. Dynamites will allow you to explode worthless items such as rocks. Thus, you won't waste any time with reeling them up. Press the upper arrow key to throw a dynamite if you catch a mole, a bone or a rock piece.

Developer

GameRival developed Gold Miner.
