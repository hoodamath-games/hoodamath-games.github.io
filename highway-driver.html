Highway Driver

By Unblocked Games

Did you know the average driver spends over 50 hours a year stuck in traffic? With over 1 billion cars on the road worldwide, traffic can get hectic! We know how desperate it is to sit in your car until the stop lights of the cars in front of you give you red eyes. But what if you could cut through traffic like a crazy F1 pilot? In Highway Driver, you'll master cutting off cars and slipping through narrow gaps.

Controls

  • Gas: W/ up arrow
  • Steer: AD or left and right arrow keys
  • Brake: S key or Spacebar
  • Change camera: C
  • Pause: Esc
  • Honk: K
  • Toggle headlights: L

How To Play Highway Driver

  • Start by selecting your ride from a lineup of 10 unique vehicles. Choose from a sleek modified coupe, a powerful muscle car, an SUV, a minivan, or classic models like the E30, E36, and E46. You can also opt for a sporty CTR, an exotic car, or even an F1 car!
  • Personalize your car to suit your style! Upgrade your handling and steering for better performance, or get creative with decals and spoilers. Experiment with different suspension levels to achieve the perfect look and feel.
  • Pick a game mode among: One Way, Two Way, Speed Bomb, and Traffic Time. Each mode has its own unique goals!
  • Decide between sunny, rainy, or night maps. Pro tip: your neon lights will shine bright on the night map, making your car look even cooler!
  • Once you've customized everything to your liking, enter the map and prepare to cut off other cars in traffic.
  • In One Way, Two Way, and Speed Bomb modes, your main goal is to drive as far as you can without crashing.
  • In Traffic Time, you'll have 100 seconds to pull off your wildest moves on a one-way road. While small bumps are okay, avoid crashes at all costs!
  • You can rack up in-game coins by performing near misses, maintaining speeds above 100 km/h, and driving against traffic.

What Sets Highway Driver Apart?

While Highway Traffic is a fan favorite on Unblocked Games, Highway Driver takes it to the next level. Enjoy a wider range of vehicles, more customization and tuning options, and a lane-tracking system that allows you to drive faster with fewer crashes!
