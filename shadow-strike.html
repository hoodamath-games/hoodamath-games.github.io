Shadow Strike

By Unblocked Games

Shadow Strike Unblocked is a 2D combat game that combines strategy, stealth, and precision. The game throws you into a maze-like battleground where your only weapon is a trusty knife. You'll aim to sneak past enemy patrols, eliminate your targets with silent melee attacks, and survive the ever-increasing challenges. If the enemies spot you, they'll waste no time shooting you down, forcing you to restart the level.

With 30 levels of escalating difficulty, Shadow Strike keeps you on your toes. Each stage introduces bigger, more complex mazes, tougher enemies, and more advanced weaponry to outsmart. Most shooting prioritize quick reflexes, but this one is a test of patience. Plan your moves carefully, stay out of sight, and strike at the perfect moment to make it through unscathed.

Controls

Point and click on the maze to move there

Small Arsenal, Big Suspense

One of the coolest things about Shadow Strike is its simplicity. Unlike some games that give you a massive arsenal, here you're equipped with just a knife. It's you versus an army of heavily armed foes, which adds a unique layer of suspense. Fans of stealth-based console games like Hitman, Metal Gear Solid, or Mark of the Ninja will feel right at home. While Shadow Strike isn't as complex as those titles, it captures the same exciting feeling of sneaking past enemies and taking them down before they even know you're there.

How to Play Shadow Strike

Mastering the art of stealth is key to winning in Shadow Strike. Here's a quick breakdown of how to play.

On computers, simply click on any point in the maze to make your character walk there.

On mobile, tap on the screen to guide your character's movement.

Avoid enemy lines of sight, marked by their yellow patrol patterns.

Use the maze to your advantage and hide behind walls, sneak around corners, and observe patrol routes before making your move.

Approach enemies silently from behind and eliminate them with a melee attack using your knife.

If an enemy spots you, they'll fire immediately. If you're hit, collect health points by standing on the + icons on the maze. And when the health drops to zero, the level restarts, so stay sharp!

Unlock New Soldiers

Completing certain levels or achieving specific objectives will bring coins to unlock new characters with unique traits like faster movements, better stealth and more. Here's a glimpse of some of the characters you can unlock:

  • The Phantom: Moves slightly faster than the default character. 500 coins to unlock.
  • Silent Shadow: Makes quieter footsteps, reducing the chance of detection in tight spaces. 800 coins to unlock.
  • Maze Master: Both quieter and faster than the default character. 1000 coins to unlock.
