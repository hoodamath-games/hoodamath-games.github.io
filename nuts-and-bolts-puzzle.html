Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

By Unblocked Games

Have you ever tried unscrewing a loose piece of furniture only to watch everything wobble and topple over? Nuts and Bolts Puzzle takes that oddly satisfying moment and turns it into a fun, unblocked game on Our Platform. Imagine a board covered in planks, each held in place by bolts. Unscrew the bolts from the planks and screw them into empty holes to drop and clear every plank from the board. But here’s the catch: you only have two minutes, and fallen planks can block the very holes you need next! It’s all about planning, quick moves, and making sure you don’t pull the wrong bolt first.

Nuts and Bolts Puzzle is super easy to learn. Use your mouse or touchpad to select the bolt you want to remove. Then click on a free hole where you want to place it. If you’re playing on a phone or tablet, just tap on the bolt, then tap on the spot you want to fill.

Nuts and Bolts Puzzle is the perfect online unblocked game for those who want both speed and smarts. So head over to Our Platform, fire up this free game, and get ready to test your puzzle-solving skills in a fun and frantic way. Good luck, and don’t forget to watch out for those sneaky blocked holes!

Control

Tap or click on the bolts, screw or unscrew

How to Play Nuts and Bolts Puzzle Unblocked

  • When the round begins, you’ll see several panels held in place by bolts. Click a bolt you want to remove, and watch the panels fall if they lose support.
  • You only have a limited number of free holes to place bolts into. If you’re too quick, you could block an important hole or trap a bolt under a fallen panel.
  • Each level grants only two minutes. Keep track of how many seconds are left, and remember you can use the Timer booster to pause the clock for 15 extra seconds of breathing room.
  • The game also provides handy boosters that you can use in various ways. Each booster can be a game-changer when used at just the right moment!
    • Hammer: Remove a plank from the board.
    • Drill: Add an extra hole where you need it most.
    • Screwdriver: Take out a screw without without needing to place it somewhere else.
    • Timer: Freeze the clock for 15 seconds.

Tips to Drop Planks Faster

  • Before you remove any bolt, visualize how the panels will fall. That extra second of thinking can save you from a big headache.
  • If you feel rushed and you’re about to make a bad move, freeze the clock! Those 15 seconds could be the difference between a perfect clear and a time-out.
  • Drilling a new hole can open up more options. This is often useful if you’ve backed yourself into a corner.
  • Save the hammer booster and only break planks that seriously block your path. You don’t want to waste this booster early on and regret it later.
  • If a plan isn’t working, switch it up. Sometimes, removing a bolt you’ve been ignoring can solve the puzzle faster.
