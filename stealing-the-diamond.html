Stealing The Diamond

By Unblocked Games

After escaping from prison, Henry finds himself back at home. He switches on the TV to a news report. And what's that thing shining so bright? Mayor Fredrickson is declaring the grand opening of the new Tunisian Diamond Exhibit at the National Museum of History and Culture. The diamond is estimated to be worth $112 million. Henry quickly considers his situation: He is broke, and his rent payments are overdue. On top of everything, he is a prison breaker. So, not much left to do but head to the museum and steal that massive diamond. (yes, that’s definitely the only way to pay rent, huh?) So, once he reaches the museum, Henry has multiple options to enter, and that’s where he’ll need your guidance.

In Stealing the Diamond, you’ll guide him through a series of choices that range from brilliant to unbelievably dumb. Sneak in like a true thief, smash your way through security, or accidentally alert every guard in a five-mile radius. No matter what happens, chaos is guaranteed! Regardless of which choice he makes, Henry ultimately succeeds in stealing the diamond. Following the successful theft of the Tunisian Diamond, Henry gains a reputation as a notable criminal. Soon this popularity will lead to him being summoned by the government for a special duty which is Infiltrating the Airship.

Stealing the Diamond Unblocked and other games of the series are available on Our Platform for players who don't want to miss any of Henry Stickmin's stories. Play now and explore the whole story.

Controls

Computers: Click to make choices

How to Play Henry Stickmin: Stealing the Diamond

  • As soon as Henry arrives at the museum, you'll need to pick your approach between two: Sneaky or loud. Point and click on the decision to watch as your choices unfold, leading to success… or an embarrassing failure. Sometimes, you will need to decide and click very quickly. So always be alert if Henry needs help with your quick reflexes.
  • You can go back and forth between decisions and experiment with different paths to unlock all the wild and unexpected endings.
  • Just remember to enjoy the absurdity because half the fun is watching Henry mess up in the most ridiculous ways.
