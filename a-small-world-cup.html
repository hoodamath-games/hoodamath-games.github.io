How to Play A Small World Cup

You need to make a few selections before entering the arena. First, choose your cup in the main menu from 8 options: World Cup Classic, World Cup 2022, Euro Cup, America Cup, Euro Clubs, Italian Clubs, English Clubs, and Other Clubs.

Next, choose your mode between Normal and Golden Goal. We'll explain the differences below on this page.

Hit Start and select from the available countries in your preferred cup. Depending on your choice, the footballers' shirt colors will change. The game will then select a random opponent for you.

Once you enter the match, it's a 1v1 situation. Both offense and defense are your responsibility! Use the ragdoll mechanics to your advantage. Aim the ball by dragging the mouse and release to slam the footballer. If you apply too much force, you might send the ball bouncing back into your own goalpost.

Score more than your opponent to move on to the next match and ultimately lift the trophy!

Practice, Normal, or Golden Goal?

There are 3 modes in A Small World Cup Unlbocked: Practice, Normal, and Golden Goal.

  • In Practice mode, there's no opposing team; it's your time to get accustomed to the ragdoll mechanics.
  • In Normal mode, each match lasts 45 seconds, and the team with the most goals wins the tournament. If you prefer a longer duration, you can change it to 90 seconds in the settings menu.
  • In Golden Goal mode, the team that scores the first goal wins the match. This mode offers quick progression but also comes with risks!
