What are Flash Games?

Flash games are first-generation web browser games that use Adobe Flash Player plugin. In the 2000s, the only way to play games on a web browser was using Flash players. Raft Wars and Siegius were a few of the most known titles of them.

When was the golden era of Flash?

Flash Player was first released in 1996 by Macromedia and acquired by Adobe in 2005. Flash games' golden era was in the mid-2000s and the early 2010s.

What happened to Flash Games?

As new web technologies like HTML5 and Javascript became common, Flash Player lost its prominence in the field. Adobe announced they'll no longer support Flash players by the end of 2020. So, most web browsers like Chrome, Firefox, and Edge stopped supporting Flash content, and it sunk into the waters of history.

Why did they disable Flash player?

Adobe had major concerns about the player's security, lack of performance and fast battery consumption. Flash lost popularity as mobiles entered the arena, and app stores and gaming websites offered more enhanced games thanks to HTML5 technology.
