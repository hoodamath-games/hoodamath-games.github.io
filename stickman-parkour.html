How to Play Stickman Parkour Unblocked

  • Your goal is to reach the squiggle at the end of each level by overcoming obstacles.
  • Use WASD, arrow keys or buttons on the screen to jump over spikes, swing across gaps using ropes, swim through water, and avoid cannonball attacks.
  • Collect golden keys to open gates and reach the squiggles.
  • Master the timing about when to act, jump, or run.
  • You'll notice game mechanics will change while swimming.
  • Explore all 30 levels and reach the end.
