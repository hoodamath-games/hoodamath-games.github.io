Army Combat

In Army Combat, you’ll be playing as a special forces agent on deployment in one of two distinct modes – Defend and Assault. Army Attack

Play in one of seven distinct locations around the world in Army Attack – from the freezing Arctic to the blazing deserts of Africa. Your objective will be to infiltrate the enemy base and realize the mission objective without alerting the enemy soldiers to your presence – or go in guns blazing, the choice is yours.

Tank War Simulator

Did you ever dream about driving a tank on the battlefield and fighting for domination? In Tank War Simulator, you’ll be leading a squadron of tanks against the enemy forces. Command your armored vehicle and destroy the enemy tanks, but don’t let your teammates get overwhelmed – good strategy is everything.

Use WASD or the arrow keys to drive your tank around. Press the left mouse button to shoot and use the right mouse button to aim. You can change between the main gun and a heavy machine gun with Q. Use the arsenal to your advantage and outsmart the enemy engineers on a dynamic battlefield.
