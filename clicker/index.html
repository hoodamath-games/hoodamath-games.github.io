Cookie Tap

Who doesn’t like cookies? Cookie Tap is a fun and straightforward clicker game, where your goal is to accumulate as many cookies as possible and become the world’s largest cookie manufacturer. You’ll be starting with nothing, clicking a big cookie button that represents manual cooking. Once you’ve collected enough cookies, upgrade your clicker or purchase passive upgrades that will help you generate cookies even when offline. Come back every once in a while to spend your cookies, upgrade your facilities, and speed up production even more.

Kick the Teddy Bear

Need to vent some negative emotions? In Kick the Teddy Bear, you get to burn away all your repressed emotions on a cartoon teddy bear, earning you money in the process. While this game isn’t idle, it’s certainly a clicker — you can spend the money you earn to purchase a whole arsenal of tools and weapons that you can use with a simple click, dealing more damage to the teddy bear and — as a result — earning you money faster. From guns to swords and chainsaws to heavy furniture, you’ll find plenty of equipment types to spice up your gameplay.

Puppet Killer

In Puppet Killer, you get to relieve stress by throwing knives at and shooting at a hanging puppet — and earning coins while you’re at it. Use the money to dress your puppet in a variety of clothing items or purchase new skins — but you can also spend it on new weapons that unlock new entertaining ways to torture the puppet. You’ll be starting with only knives, but you’ll soon unlock automatic weapons, explosives, and even futuristic laser weaponry.

Ape NFT Generator

Did you ever want to have your own NFT with a cute monkey, but didn’t want to spend the exorbitant amount of money it often requires? In Ape NFT Generator, you can generate your own Ape and upgrade it, building an NFT empire. Upgrade your website, hire new artists, and build an international community around your NFT. Soon, you won’t have to click anymore and will be earning money passively — but clicking will also increase your multiplier, which will also double your passive production. 

Nova Idle Clicker

A unique idle game from Our Platform, Nova Idle Clicker combines two games into one — a clicker and a block breaker. As you click on the oncoming squares and triangles, you’ll also be shooting cannonballs to help you clear the level faster. Upgrade your clicking power and your balls, as each level will be harder than the previous one.
