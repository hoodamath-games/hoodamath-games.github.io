Edelweiss

By Unblocked Games

Edelweiss is a 3D exploration-based platformer game unblocked. Find yourself as a little girl in the jungle, climb through all the walls to find stamina hidden in Edelweiss flowers. Learn new climbing techniques, improve stamina and enjoy the journey in the jungle. You'll have the helpers. Turn on volume to play with calming sounds in this no-rush environment.

Controls

Computers

  • Move: WASD/Arrow keys
  • Jump/Interact: Spacebar

Mobiles

  • Move by dragging the virtual joystick on the screen
  • Jump by tapping the action button

How to Play Edelweiss?

  • Enter a username and start playing. Move using WASD or arrow keys. Movement changes relative to the camera angle.
  • Jump by pressing the spacebar.
  • Make use of bushes or rocks to climb to higher places.
  • If you fall from too high, you need to start over.
  • Collect staminas hidden on the map to improve your climbing and unlock new talents.

Get Help From Grandmas

Grandmas are town people who used to climb those walls very long time ago. Make use of their knowledge and experience.

Approach them and press space to talk with them. They'll answer your questions about how to play Edelweiss.

Meet Campers

Campers are a good way to save progress. Go and say hello to campers when you'd like to use them as a checkpoint. This way, when you fall down from high, you can start over from the last camper you met.

They also offer you some water, new talents, or stamina.

Just press space to interact with them.

Stay Away From Water

The climber is a little kid who doesn't know how to swim yet. So it's better to stay away from rivers, lakes and waterfalls. Otherwise, you'll go back to the last camper.

Multiplayer Climbing

If you'd like to play the game with a friend, you can play Edelweiss multiplayer. Just refresh the gamepass in the main menu, copy it, and send it to your friend. Invite your friends and have an unblocked game party together at school.
