What are Skill Games?

Skill games (also known as skill-based games) have challenging mechanics that require players to maintain focus, respond, and adapt quickly, showcasing fine motor skills and hand-eye coordination. In these games, luck plays a minimal role, if any. Players may go through a few trials and errors to adapt to gameplay. Despite its initially frustrating nature, skill-based games will become an addiction once you explore them further.

Top Unblocked Skill Games

Friday Night Funkin

If you're looking for an unblocked rhythm game that has some fantastic 90s vibes, freestyle music, a cute storyline, plenty of Vocaloid and new jazz beats, and so much more, Friday Night Funkin should be at the top of your list. With fantastic B-Boy graphics, Guitar Hero-esque game mechanics and fast-paced note sequences, don't forget to set this game on easy mode when you first fire it up!

All you have to do is hit the notes with the corresponding arrow keys and beat the levels as you go. One of the unique things about FNF is that once completing the tutorial, you'll compete with different characters in story mode, with six unique week competitions.

Playing the boyfriend, you must show your girlfriend that you are a wildly talented musician and win her heart by beating an array of funky characters.

Tap Tap Shots

Games with tapping mechanics won't always fall into the skill category, but Tap Tap Shots takes more than mindless clicking to win the day. Get addicted to shooting hoops by adjusting your angle and tapping for height to get the ball in the basket.

Too many taps will result in too much air - and when the name of the game is scoring as many baskets as you can before the time runs out, you'll want to ensure you successfully land as many as possible.

Keep an eye on the bar at the top of the screen to keep track of time, score big with perfect aim, tie together successive perfect shots, and set your ball aflame for top-scoring opportunities. The great news is that all you'll need to get started is your mouse.

Geometry Dash

Geometry Dash is one of the most popular arcade games to be released in the last decade, and you can now play this fantastic title with an unblocked Our Platform twist. Feel the rhythm and take the heat as you tap, jump and fly your cube across a dynamic obstacle-filled landscape.

The music really is the most fantastic aspect of the game, and the rhythm will help you complete multiple game modes and various themes - so don't forget to turn it up!

Drift Boss

Challenge your skills and reflexes as a professional drift driver in the fantastic Drift Boss game. Players will not only need to control their car, increase their speed and then release drifts as they zoom around the track, but they can also follow special techniques and leverage boosters to score more points.

This game combines great graphics and fantastic sound effects on a challenging trackway, so it's not going to be as easy as it may look at first glance.

Tap Tap Goals

Just like its basketball brethren, if you like tapping games, Tap Tap Goals combines skill and clicking on a countdown for addictive fun. The more goals you score, the more points you'll get, and you'll even get additional seconds on the timer when you succeed on target. This game brings some extra challenges with obstacles, such as grey blocks and bombs that will slow you down.

Stickjet Challenge

Take your little stickman ninja for a ride across an array of levels as you jump and jetpack from A to B to complete the Stickjet Challenge. Make sure that this little guy progresses without touching anything with red lines and avoiding obstacles that will try to get in your way. If you don't stick to the green squares, you'll go back to the start, so take off with your jetpack and fly for glory.

OvO

OvO is another stickman game that will take arcade skills and lightning-fast reflexes to complete. Players will be expected to protect their little stickman from falling down, with the aim of reaching the final flag by jumping, smashing, diving and sliding.

Don't be fooled by the basic graphics; this game has unique obstacles, a timer, an array of controls for special movements and a whopping 61 levels that are still being added to as we speak.
