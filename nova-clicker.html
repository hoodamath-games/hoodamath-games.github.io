Nova Clicker

By Unblocked Games

Nova Clicker Unblocked takes you on an interstellar adventure in a distant galaxy where all the Novas live in peace. You must have met Our Platform before, the mascot of Unblocked Games. He'll hold your hand and introduce you to all the other Novas he knows. All you must do is click on Our Platform to generate stars. As you progress, unlock new and exciting versions of Nova, including Police Nova, King Nova, Funky Nova, Immortal Nova, and many more!
Each Nova adds a fresh layer of fun as you collect stars faster, unlock upgrades, and discover unique backgrounds like Moon, Mars, and Light Speed.
With golden novas providing bonus stars and the x2 button speeding up your clicks, Nova Clicker is the ultimate cosmic clicker game!

Controls

Computers
Click on the Nova to generate stars

Mobiles
Tap on the Nova to generate stars

How to Play Nova Clicker

  • Start with Our Platform and click to create stars.
  • Use stars to buy clickers and boosters using the shop at the right side of your screen.
  • As you create more stars you'll be able to buy more advanced star generators such as nova booster, nova bomb and even Nova Asteroid.
  • As you progress you can unlock new novas, like baby Nova, Police Nova, King Nova, and others, and discover powerful abilities.
  • From time to time, you'll see Golden Novas passing through your screen. Catch and click on them to earn enourmous amounts of stars.
  • If you don't like waiting while your generators work hard to create more, you can speed things up by clicking the x2 button.
  • One of the great features of Nova Clicker is that as you progress, the game will take you new vibrant lands like moon, mars and Star Rain to keep the excitement going.

Ascend Your Novas

Once you've made significant progress and unlocked most items in the game, it's time to ascend your Novas. By ascending, you'll reset your game and start a new branch in the Nova Generation Business. Though you'll begin from scratch, you'll retain all bonuses and benefit from the ascension multiplier, allowing you to progress much faster than your initial playthrough.

Features

  • Unlockable Novas: Unlock and play with unique Novas like Police Nova, King Nova, Funky Nova, Immortal Nova, and more!
  • Dynamic Backgrounds: Discover and unlock vibrant cosmic scenes such as Star Rain to enhance the visual experience.
  • Golden Novas: Randomly appearing Golden Novas give you bonus stars when clicked.
  • Ascension System: Reset your progress to start anew with powerful bonuses and an ascension multiplier for faster advancement.
  • Engaging Music and Sound Effects: Enjoy fun, immersive sound effects and music as you click your way to success.
  • x2 Speed Boost: Hit the x2 button to double your clicking speed and skyrocket your star generation.
